A Warm Welcome

London De Academy is an accredited education provider located in the heart of London. We are close to the London School of Economics, (LSE) and St Pauls Cathedral, an iconic landmark of British history. We offer Ofqual approved undergraduate and postgraduate qualifications, delivered by qualified teachers with rigorous academic standards. We focus on supporting our students in realising their true potential. Most students choose us because we help them to build the skills needed in todays interconnected global economy.

Consider us as your learning partner and we look forward to working in partnership with you to achieve your highest career prospects. Whatever is your interest or your ambition, we can help you to find the right pathway. We are delighted that you are considering a life-changing investment in your future. There is an opportunity waiting for you here at London De Academy, along with unrivalled learning experiences.
