About Us

We offer a range of Ofqual recognised qualifications including certificates, undergraduate & postgraduate diplomas to the UK and international students. We use modern and innovative technology to deliver courses with lessons, assignments, resources and study materials. These programmes will genuinely enhance employability skills and increase life opportunities.

We have upheld a rigorous commitment to the professional development of students and professionals to discover their intellectual career passion through our programmes. Our experienced and qualified instructors are available to guide you every step of the way, working towards an internationally recognised qualification.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be recognised as a leading and most innovative lifelong learning institute that delivers affordable education and training to career-minded professionals, individuals and progressive enterprises.

To be the most innovative and technologically advanced institute to provide education

To be recognised beyond the UK as a premier education provider of LONDON

To be the most recommended provider in the market to help individuals and businesses to improve knowledge and skills in the area of Business Management, Cyber Security, Supply Chain Management and Entrepreneurship.

Our Mission

The principal aims of London De Academy are to:

Provide flexible first-class higher education courses which meet the changing educational, cultural, personal and career needs of students of all ages, participants, partners, vendors and clients

Endeavour to provide individuals from diverse social and educational backgrounds, a superior learning experience to lead a successful and impactful career

Ensure the London De Academy, provides an inclusive working and learning environment for its students and staff so that all may develop to their full potential

Develop sustainable partnerships within the UK and beyond to increase opportunities for knowledge sharing, work placement and hybrid learning system

Develop the capacity to respond rapidly to new and changing opportunities in higher and further education

Trained individuals for the professional organisational skills to win an employment contract

Maintain and develop excellence in education and provide the highest quality training in all our subject areas

Build our reputation in the industry to be known as an academic and corporate education provider
