Overview

The course is designed to provide candidates of any background with a well-rounded understanding of Blockchain basics and its key components. The program provides a foundational understanding of how blockchain works and how companies use its potential to solve real business problems. The foundation program tour across the inner workings and mechanics of the blockchain by explaining concepts like decentralisation, peer-to-peer networking, smart contract automation, and many more. The course acts as a one-stop dictionary of blockchain explaining important blockchain principles in brief terms while also providing a comprehensive list of additional resources that you can reference afterwards.

Who is it for?

1. The blockchain foundation program is open for anyone who is new to the technology and anticipates a better understanding of its structure and applications. This includes students, graduates, marketers, designers, business analysts or investors. Blockchain Foundation Programme is benefit for the students who are intend to follow their Blockchain Bachelor Degrees at the University level and also covering individuals who are already familiar with blockchain and also who would like to learn more about the industry and explore where it’s heading.


The ideal student for this course is someone who wants to quickly understand and expand their knowledge of how blockchain and Bitcoin work as well as how they are applied in business and it future.
2. Business people who want to learn more about how blockchain and Bitcoin are impacting the world of business.

Course Outlines

- Introduction to Blockchain - Technology Overview

- Blockchain vs Cryptocurrency “BITCOIN” - Blockchain-Non-Technical Technology Overview (Digital Tokens, DeFi, Smart Contract, Consensus, Bitcoin Mining, and Security etc...)

- Blockchain Ecosystem

- Benefits of Blockchain & Solution – Mythologies & Facts

- Use Cases of Blockchain

- Industry Adoption (Who is using it and for what?)

- How Do I get started? Implementation and Cost

- Future of Blockchain and Industry outlook
