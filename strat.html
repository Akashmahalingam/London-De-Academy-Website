ACCREDITATION SERVICES FOR INTERNATIONAL COLLEGES (ASIC)
London De Academy is fully inspected and accredited by ASIC, an independent accreditation organisation approved by the UK Visas & Immigration (UKVI), part of the Home Office, as a qualifying requirement for UK institutions wishing to enrol international students (from outside the European Economic Area (EEA) and Switzerland) on the Short-term study visa and Standard Visitor visa. ASIC is listed in the Council for Higher Education Accreditation (CHEA) in the US and a member of the CHEA International Quality Group (CIQG). It is also a member of the British Quality Foundation (BQF), the European Association for Quality Assurance in Higher Education (ENQA), the Global Universities in Distance Education Association (GUIDE), the Association of International Education Administrators (AIEA) and the European Distance and E-Learning Network (EDEN).
LONDON SCHOOL OF BUSINESS & ENTREPRENEURSHIP
This is another initiative of London De Academy, to join hands with London School of Business & Entrepreneurship as a highly trusted academic partner to create, deliver and execute learning experiences to drive higher levels of engagement. We believe exceptional people's development relies on providing exceptional experiences. With the help and support of our strategic alliance, we’ll help individuals to take their skills and talent onto the next level.
NOVUS EDUCATION GROUP
NOVUS is one of the leading consultancy services and education advisors who provide guidance and support to students and professionals across the globe. This enables them to achieve their full potential and gain access to the world’s top universities and colleges. This is a unique business model that offers a range of educational services from marketing to accreditation and manages international study centres on behalf of strategic and academic partners.
LONDON MANAGEMENT QUALIFICATIONS
The London Management Qualifications (LMQ) is a UK based awarding organisation credit rated by SCQF validating a set of innovative programmes covering a range of levels of study.
WHD
World Humanitarian Drive (WHD) is an International NGO with operations in 12 countries, founded in Croydon, U.K. by British Indian Global Peace activist, Entrepreneur, Writer, Dr Abdul Basit Syed.
The primary vision of WHD is to promote Peace, Education and Trade Harmony initiatives globally.
